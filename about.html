About BOMA Student Council

Dedicated to Student Mental Health & Wellbeing

Our Mission

BOMA Student Council is dedicated to creating a supportive and inclusive environment where every student's mental health and wellbeing is prioritized. We believe in breaking down barriers to mental health support and fostering a community of understanding and compassion.

Our Values

  • Confidentiality and Trust
  • Inclusive Support
  • Student Empowerment
  • Mental Health Advocacy
